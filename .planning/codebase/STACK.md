# Technology Stack

## Core Framework
- **Framework**: [SvelteKit](https://kit.svelte.dev/) (v2.21+)
- **UI Library**: [Svelte](https://svelte.dev/) (v5.33+)
- **Language**: [TypeScript](https://www.typescriptlang.org/) (v5.7+)

## Styling & Design
- **CSS Framework**: [Tailwind CSS](https://tailwindcss.com/) (v4.1+)
- **Typography**: `@tailwindcss/typography`
- **Icons**: [Lucide Svelte](https://lucide.dev/guide/svelte)

## Backend & Integration
- **Backend as a Service (BaaS)**: [Appwrite](https://appwrite.io/)
- **SDKs**: `appwrite` (browser) and `node-appwrite` (server-side)

## Build & Tools
- **Build Tool**: [Vite](https://vitejs.dev/) (v6.3+)
- **Package Manager**: `pnpm` (inferred from `pnpm-lock.yaml`)
- **Linting/Checking**: `svelte-check`
